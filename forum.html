U.S ON DRUGS

U.S On Drugs is a fictional event consisting of a compilation of exhibitions, conferences, screenings, performances, workshops and more. All surrounding the issue of the war on drugs in the United States.

Through this week-long forum, visitors confront addiction issues and prevention tactics in the country’s history. Immersing themselves in the politics of the war, the lives of those affected by it, and the historical elements that construct the issue leading to its current state.
