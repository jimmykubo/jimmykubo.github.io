DICEY MAGAZINE

Dicey Mag. was created in 2023 as a design assignment initiated by Johan Van Looveren at the department of Media & Information Design at Luca School of Arts in Brussels. All texts come from medium.com, an open source platform.

The objective was to develop a contemporary magazine design that effectively integrates and showcases open-source content, reflecting current trends in media and design.
