ABOUT

Jimmy Kubo, born in São Paulo, Brazil, studied graphic design at ESA Saint-Luc Institute of Brussels, where he worked on a diverse range of printed and digital media. He also completed a preparatory program in the department of Media & Information Design at LUCA School of Arts, focusing on affective visualisation, storytelling, and interactive design.
